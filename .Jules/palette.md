## 2024-06-11 - Add ARIA Labels to Tuning Buttons and Frequency Input
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons or interactive inputs without distinct labels (such as up/down tuning arrows) must have explicit `aria-label` attributes to inform screen reader users of their purpose.
**Action:** Always ensure that any icon-only interactive controls and unlabeled form inputs receive descriptive ARIA labels to maintain basic screen reader accessibility.
